Pizza

Pizza, a dish of Italian origin, has become a universally popular and versatile food enjoyed across cultures and continents. Pizza traditionally includes a flattened disk of bread dough. It is topped with ingredients such as olive oil, oregano, tomatoes or tomato sauce, mozzarella or other cheeses, and various other toppings. It is typically baked at high temperatures, often in a wood-fired oven, and served hot.









Pastries

Pastries are a type of baked food made from a dough that consists of flour, water, and shortening. They can be sweet or savory and are often used as a base for various dishes or desserts. Common types of pastries include croissants, danishes, tarts, and pies. They are known for their flaky texture and rich flavor, making them a favorite in many cuisines.
